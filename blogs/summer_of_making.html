Hack Club Summer of Making (GitHub-sponsored)

Summer 2025

I participated in the Hack Club Summer of Making, an initiative sponsored by GitHub. During the 3-month period, I logged 450+ tracked coding hours across 4+ shipped open-source projects.

Because of this work, I was ranked in the top tier on a competitive global leaderboard and was awarded a $1,649 laptop (RTX 5070, 32GB RAM, Ryzen AI 7) as a merit-based prize.

The experience involved rapidly learning and applying new technologies across each open-source project.
