Running a Real Product on $7/Month

Vardhman Electricals  ·  Nov 2025 – Jun 2026  ·  By Yogya Chugh

This article is currently being written. Check back soon for the full story.

The story of building Vardhman Saathi — a comprehensive business suite and salesperson credit system using TypeScript, Electron, and Hono — and running the entire product on $7/month infrastructure from November 2025 to June 2026.

“You don’t need a data center. You need the right architecture.”

The stack: Supabase (database + auth), Cloudflare R2 (file storage), Render (backend hosting). Desktop app in Electron + TypeScript. Android app sharing the same codebase. Visit the live product at vardhmansaathi.shop ↗.
